Open-Face Pineapple Chinese Poker

How to Play:

Each player has to build 3 poker hands.

  • Hand 1 (top) uses 3 cards, must be the weakest hand.
  • Hand 2 (middle) uses 5 cards, and must be stronger than the top hand.
  • Hand 3 (bottom) uses 5 cards, and must be the strongest hand played.
If the hand strength does not increase in descending order, the player fouls.

Game Play:

There are a few approaches on how to play, so to try to keep things as fair as possible:

Each player is dealt 5 cards.

Taking turns, each plays places a card in 1 of their 3 hands.

After all the cards are placed, taking turns, each player draws 3 cards, plays 2 and discards 1, until all hards are finished.
